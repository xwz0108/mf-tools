The three leading Jamstack deployment platforms compared on pricing, performance, and developer experience.

### When to Choose Each

**Vercel** — Best for Next.js projects. Deep integration with the framework. Excellent DX with automatic preview URLs and instant rollbacks.

**Netlify** — Best for static sites and forms. Netlify Forms is unique (no backend needed for form submissions). Great for Gatsby and Hugo sites.

**Cloudflare Pages** — Best for global performance. Cloudflare CDN covers 330+ cities. Unlimited bandwidth on free plan. Best for high-traffic sites on a budget.

### Our Pick

For most developers: Vercel wins for Next.js. Cloudflare Pages wins for raw speed. Netlify wins if you need forms.
