## How to Choose the Right AI Tool

With hundreds of AI tools available, choosing can be overwhelming. Here is a framework.

### Step 1: Define Your Use Case

| Use Case | Best Tool Type |
|----------|---------------|
| Writing content | AI Writers (Jasper, Writesonic) |
| Creating images | AI Image Gen (Midjourney, Leonardo) |
| Writing code | AI Coding (Cursor, Copilot) |
| SEO optimization | AI SEO (Surfer, Frase) |
| Video creation | AI Video (Runway, Pika) |

### Step 2: Compare Key Factors

| Factor | What to Check |
|--------|--------------|
| Price | Monthly cost vs. value delivered |
| Free tier | Can you test before paying? |
| Output quality | Read reviews, not just marketing |
| Learning curve | How fast can you get productive? |
| Integration | Does it work with your existing tools? |

### Step 3: Calculate ROI

Use a commission calculator to estimate earnings if you plan to monetize. For affiliate marketers, prioritize tools with recurring commissions.

### Step 4: Test Before Committing

Most tools offer free trials or free tiers. Test 2-3 options before subscribing. What works for others may not work for you.
